Virtual Conductor

Setup

To ensure that your hand gestures are recognised, keep your hands at a distance of about 50cm (20 inches) from the camera.
Make sure to avoid bright lights in the background, as they could interfere with the gesture recognition.

Instructions

Control each instrument separately by making hand gestures inside the rectangle area belonging to that instrument.
The color of the circles in the corners of each area indicate whether the instrument is playing, using red/green color.
The number indicates which style they are playing in.

Gesture Action
Thumbs up Turn the instrument on
Thumbs down Turn the instrument off
Index finger up Switch to instrument intensity 1
Index and long finger up Switch to instrument intensity 2
